Meet The Founder

Julia Idaewor is a part-time, self-taught makeup artist based out of Dallas, Texas. She graduated from the University of Georgia in May 2019 with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. While in college, she reignited her passion for enhancing beauty and self-confidence through makeup and started her makeup artistry serving multiple clients in the Metro Atlanta area. Though makeup is her part-time job, she is always passionate about bolstering women's self-confidence, inner, and outer beauty by making them feel beautiful from the inside out!
